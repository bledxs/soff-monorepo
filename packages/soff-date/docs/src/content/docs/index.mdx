```typescript

// Get all holidays for 2025
```

## What's Included?

- **Fixed Dates**: December 25 (Christmas)
- **Nth Weekday**: 3rd Monday of January (MLK Day)
- **Easter-Relative**: Good Friday (Easter - 2 days)
- **Custom Calculations**: Your own logic
- **Shift Rules**: Move holidays from weekends to weekdays
- **Business Days**: Skip weekends and holidays
- **i18n Support**: English, Spanish, Portuguese
